ABOUT ME

I am a hardworking and passionate Computer Science student at Florida Tech with Junior standing. I plan to specialize in Machine Learning and Neural Networks, and plan to use these tools to help those with disabilities or who have disadvantages when it comes to using technology.

Currently, I am working as a Math Tutor with Space Coast Academics, where I help middle and high school students with improving their mathematics skills. I also conduct research at Florida Tech with Dr. White through his NEural TransmissionS(NETS) Lab, where I use machine learning and other forms of artificial intelligence in a research setting. Through this research I have done signal processing and presented at the 2024 MAA-FL Conference where I received the Best Presentation Award for Undergraduate Students.
